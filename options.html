How to use

Shortcuts

  1. Press Cmd + . (Mac) or Ctrl + . (Windows/Linux) to open the popup
  2. Press the configured key to navigate to the URL
  3. You can also click on a shortcut in the popup

Redirect Rules

  1. Add a rule with a URL pattern and a destination URL
  2. When you visit a URL that matches the pattern, you'll be automatically redirected
  3. Use Contains for simple text matching, Wildcard for glob patterns (*), or Regex for advanced patterns
  4. Toggle rules on/off without deleting them
